I presented research on parameter identifiability in biological dynamical systems using Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs) and Kolmogorov–Arnold Networks (KANs). The talk discussed the challenges of recovering unknown parameters from sparse and noisy observations and compared the performance of PINNs and KANs for inverse problems in mathematical biology.
